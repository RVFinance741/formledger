Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 29, 2026  ·  Applies to FormLedger web app and Android app

The short version: FormLedger does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Everything you enter stays on your device. We have no access to your workout data, profile, or any information you provide.

1. Who We Are

FormLedger is a precision workout tracking application available at formledger.ca and on the Google Play Store. FormLedger is operated as an independent product.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

FormLedger is designed with a local-first, privacy-first architecture. We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Workout sessions, exercise data, or training history
  • Body weight, BMI, or health metrics you enter
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter into FormLedger — including your profile, workout history, personal records, and preferences — is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server operated by FormLedger or any third party.

If you uninstall the app or clear your browser data, your FormLedger data will be permanently deleted from your device. We cannot recover it.

4. Third-Party Services

The FormLedger app loads the following third-party resources when you first open it:

  • Google Fonts — used to load the Syne and DM Mono typefaces. Google may log your IP address as part of this request. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Chart.js (jsDelivr CDN) — used to render progress charts. jsDelivr may log your IP address. See jsDelivr's Privacy Policy.

After the first load, FormLedger works entirely offline and makes no further external requests.

5. Children's Privacy

FormLedger is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Because we collect no data from any users, there is no specific risk to minors.

6. Your Rights

Because FormLedger does not collect or store any personal data on external servers, there is no personal data held by us to access, correct, export, or delete. Your data exists only on your device and is entirely within your control.

You can delete all FormLedger data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or uninstalling the app.

7. Security

Since no personal data is transmitted to or stored on external servers, there is no central database that could be breached. Your data security is determined by the security of your own device.

8. Changes to This Policy

If FormLedger's data practices change in the future — for example, if account features or cloud sync are introduced — this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features launch. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us via the FormLedger website at formledger.ca.
